Project vision

A cleaner approach to presenting engineering.

Aether is presented as a research-driven fixed-wing platform focused on autonomy, development discipline and future scalability. The goal is not only to document progress, but to communicate a strong technological identity.

Platform

Designed for efficient autonomous flight.

The Aether platform explores fixed-wing efficiency, autonomous development workflows and experimental validation through a research-focused aerial system.
